Q&A

IS THERE A GIFT REGISTRY?

Yes, it is linked on our wedding website under "Registry!" We also have a "Honeymoon Fund" that you can donate to as well!

WHEN IS THE RSVP DEADLINE?

Please RSVP no later than March 1, 2025. If we do not receive an RSVP by that date, we will assume you are not able to attend, and we will not have a seat for you during the reception.

WHAT TIME SHOULD I ARRIVE?

Most of our guests are traveling 1hr+ to be with us on our big day! Please consider traffic on your way to our venue; the ceremony will begin at 3:30 PM. Please try to arrive no later than 3:15 PM.

WHERE SHOULD I PARK?

There will be someone from the venue working the parking area to direct you. Be prepared to walk through grass as that is where the parking area is located!

CAN I BRING A DATE/PLUS ONE?

We unfortunately cannot accommodate additional guests. Due to limited capacity, we can only allow the people named on the save the dates/invites.

ARE KIDS WELCOME?

While we love all of your little ones, we have decided to keep our wedding and reception an adults-only event. We hope you understand and enjoy a night out! Please reach out if you have any questions.

IS THE WEDDING INDOORS OR OUTDOORS?

The ceremony and reception will take place indoors along with the cocktail hour!

WHAT SHOULD I WEAR?

We request a semi-formal dress code. This means cocktail, semi-formal, or formal dress/Sarees. Please no white clothing, daytime/informal dresses, or jeans.

WHAT KIND OF SHOES SHOULD I WEAR?

Since everything is indoors, feel free to wear what is comfortable for you. Keep in mind our request for semi-formal/formal attire!

ARE THERE ANY COLORS TO AVOID?

Please avoid colors traditionally reserved for the bride (white, ivory, cream) and the wedding party colors (gem and wine).

CAN I TAKE PICTURES DURING THE CEREMONY?

We are having an “unplugged” ceremony, so we ask that all phones and devices be put away and on silent. Feel free to take photos after the ceremony!

IS THERE A HOTEL NEAR THE VENUE?

Yes! Please refer to the travel section on the website for a list of local hotels near our venue.
